July 11, 2025 - Thoughts and Thoughts

Yet another day where I do absolutly nothing. Well, to be fair I did do some Real analysis and analytic number theory, well by "did" I meant I just read 2 pages and left. This lazy-ambitious personality has been so wildly demotivating I can't even escape it. I don't know why but whenever I try escaping it, I end up where I began. July 13, 2025 - Expectation

It seems to me that expectation is the number one thief of happiness — number two, of course, being comparison. You expect something to happen, and it doesn't go accordingly, and then your big fat smiley face turns into a big ugly sad face. The disappointment you feel after it is greatly unmeasurable.

"We must learn to live without expectations, not because it's easier, but because it's wiser."
— Unknown

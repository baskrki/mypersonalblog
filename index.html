July 14, 2025 - Failing

When you get bad feelings about things not going accordingly as planned, you just imagine the worst that can happen to you and move on from there. It just reduces your anxiety by a lot.

"If you want to control your anxiety, start by accepting the worst that could happen. Then improve upon it."
— Dale Carnegie

July 13, 2025 - Expectation

It seems to me that expectation is the number one thief of happiness — number two, of course, being comparison. You expect something to happen, and it doesn't go accordingly, and then your big fat smiley face turns into a big ugly sad face. The disappointment you feel after it is greatly unmeasurable.

"We must learn to live without expectations, not because it's easier, but because it's wiser."
— Unknown

July 11, 2025 - Thoughts and Thoughts

Yet another day where I do absolutely nothing. Well, to be fair, I did do some real analysis and analytic number theory — well, by "did" I meant I just read 2 pages and left. This lazy-ambitious personality has been so wildly demotivating I can't even escape it. I don't know why, but whenever I try escaping it, I end up where I began. I promise myself I'll do better, but 2 days later, there I am with my unfulfilled promises.

Loneliness is yet another factor in my life that has been constantly poking me. Maybe God is keeping something for me, and I hope it's not a giant middle finger. Speaking of God, is there even such a thing called "God"? I mean, Gödel did make an attempt to prove it, but I've been lingering about it.

“I don't believe in empirical science. I only believe in a priori truth.”
— Kurt Gödel
